Dauphin Real Estate

“Designed while working at Dauphin Real Estates. All rights belong to the original brand.”

For Dauphin Real Estate, I independently designed and developed the full website using Framer, managing the complete process from concept to live launch. I structured the user experience, designed the interface, and integrated Dubai property listings to ensure smooth browsing and clear presentation of real estate projects.
